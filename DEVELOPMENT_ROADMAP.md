## 🏎️ Phase 3: Hardware Optimization
### 3.1 The Integrated Graphics Challenge
*   **Discovery**: Identified that the system was running on an **11th Gen Intel i3** with Integrated Graphics (no CUDA support).
*   **Optimization**: Removed GPU-heavy `bitsandbytes` (4-bit) configurations to prevent crashes.

### 3.2 Intel OpenVINO "Turbo" Mode
*   **Upgrade**: Integrated **Intel OpenVINO** and **Optimum Intel**.
*   **Result**: Shifted inference from the standard CPU core to the specialized AI-accelerated instruction set, providing a massive speed boost for the i3 processor.
